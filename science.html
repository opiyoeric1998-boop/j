Our Science Program

At Jets Elementary, we nurture young scientists through discovery-based learning aligned with the CBC curriculum. Our program develops curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills that prepare students for a rapidly changing world. We believe every child is a natural scientist, and our role is to provide the tools and environment for that scientist to flourish.

Our Science Approach

We believe in making science engaging, relevant, and fun for every child

Hands-on Learning

Practical experiments and activities that make science come alive. Students learn by doing, not just by listening.

Critical Thinking

Encourage questioning, prediction, and analysis. We teach students how to think, not what to think.

Environment Focus

Teach care for nature and sustainable practices. Students learn to be responsible stewards of our planet.

Collaboration

Group projects and team-based discovery. Learning to work together is a crucial scientific skill.
