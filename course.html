Learning & Enrichment Programmes

A safe, engaging, and nurturing environment where your child continues to learn, play, and grow after the school day ends.

Safe Learning Beyond School Hours

Our Aftercare Services provide a structured, safe, and stimulating environment for children after regular school hours. We understand the challenges working parents face, and we're here to support both you and your child.

At Jets Elementary, aftercare isn't just supervision—it's an extension of our educational philosophy. We create opportunities for social interaction, skill development, and creative expression in a caring, secure setting.

Safety & Security

Professional staff ensure your child's wellbeing at all times with secure facilities and supervised activities.

Skill Development

Activities designed to build confidence, critical thinking, and new abilities in a supportive environment.

Social Growth

Opportunities to build friendships, learn teamwork, and develop social skills through guided interactions.

Our Aftercare Programmes

Each programme is carefully designed to nurture different aspects of your child's development

Sports and Play

Active games and team sports that promote physical fitness, coordination, and social skills. Our supervised play areas allow children to release energy in a safe, structured way.

  • Builds teamwork and sportsmanship
  • Improves physical health and coordination
  • Teaches discipline and following rules
  • Encourages healthy competition

Computer Learning & Robotics

Introduction to technology, coding basics, and simple robotics projects. We make digital learning fun and accessible for young minds.

  • Develops problem-solving skills
  • Introduces basic programming concepts
  • Encourages creativity and innovation
  • Builds confidence with technology

Chess Game

The timeless game of strategy taught in an engaging, age-appropriate way. Children learn to think ahead and develop mental focus.

  • Enhances critical thinking skills
  • Improves concentration and patience
  • Teaches planning and strategy
  • Boosts memory and cognitive abilities

Swimming

Supervised swimming lessons and water safety instruction. Our certified instructors ensure a safe, enjoyable introduction to aquatic skills.

  • Teaches essential water safety skills
  • Builds confidence in the water
  • Improves overall physical fitness
  • Develops coordination and strength

Cooking

Simple, safe cooking activities that teach basic kitchen skills and nutrition. Children learn to prepare healthy snacks while understanding food concepts.

  • Teaches practical life skills
  • Encourages healthy eating habits
  • Develops math and measuring skills
  • Boosts creativity and confidence

Debate

Age-appropriate discussion and public speaking activities that help children express their ideas clearly and respectfully.

  • Improves communication skills
  • Builds confidence in public speaking
  • Teaches respectful disagreement
  • Develops critical thinking and reasoning

Taekwondo

Martial arts training focused on discipline, respect, and self-control rather than aggression. Our certified instructors emphasize character development.

  • Promotes self-discipline and focus
  • Teaches respect for self and others
  • Builds confidence and self-esteem
  • Improves physical coordination
