About Jets Elementary

Nurturing young minds with excellence, compassion, and innovation

Our Story

Jets Elementary School was founded in August 2023 by its visionary founder, Madam Diannah Omariba, with a clear mission: to provide a nurturing, learning centered environment where every child has the opportunity to grow, discover, and succeed. Jets opened its door with an initial enrollment of 50 learners.

Despite being a young instituition, the school quickly demonstrated its commitment to excellence. Within just two months of operation, jets proudly celebrated its first graduating learner, making a significance milestone in its early journey. Today jets elementary stands as a growing international minded instituition, committed to excellence, inclusivity, and preparing young learners to thrive in global community.

What makes Jets Elementary unique is our holistic approach to education. We believe that true learning extends beyond textbooks and classrooms. Our curriculum integrates academics with life skills, creativity, and social responsibility, preparing our students not for exams, but for life itself.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, student-centered education that empowers children to become confident, compassionate, and responsible global citizens. We strive to create a learning environment that celebrate diversity, encourage critical thinking, and fosters a lifelong love of learning.

Our Vision

We envision a school where every child discover their unique talents, develops strong character, and acquires the skils needed to thrive in an ever changing world. Our vision is to be a leading elementary school known for academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and a strong sense of community.
