Additional Information

We provide additional services to support our students' holistic development and convenience for parents.

Transport Services

We offer safe and reliable school transport services covering major routes in and around Kisumu. Our vehicles are well-maintained and driven by experienced, certified drivers.

  • Designated pick-up and drop-off points
  • Trained matrons on board
  • GPS-tracked vehicles
  • Affordable termly transport fees

Meals Programme

We provide nutritious, balanced meals prepared in our modern, hygienic kitchen. Our meals programme follows guidelines from nutritionists to support children's growth and learning.

  • Breakfast and lunch provided
  • Special dietary needs accommodated
  • Monthly menu shared with parents
  • Emphasis on fruits and vegetables
