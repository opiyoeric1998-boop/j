Our Educational Programmes

At Jets Elementary, we offer a holistic curriculum that nurtures young minds academically, socially, and emotionally through diverse, well-structured programmes.

CBC-Aligned Learning

Our curriculum follows the Competency Based Curriculum framework, focusing on developing practical skills, creativity, and active learner participation. We emphasize real-world application of knowledge.

Modern ICT Exposure

Learners develop digital confidence through hands-on experience with computers and modern learning tools. Our ICT programme builds essential problem-solving and technological skills for the future.

Safe and Caring Environment

We prioritize emotional well-being alongside academic growth. Our school provides secure surroundings with friendly, supportive teachers who offer guidance and personalized attention to each child.

Balanced Co-curricular Activities

Through sports, music, arts, clubs, and games, we support physical, social, and creative development. These activities help build teamwork, discipline, and well-rounded personalities.

Play-Based Early Years Programme

Specifically designed for PP1, PP2, and lower primary, this approach uses play, exploration, and creativity to build foundational skills. Learning is fun, engaging, and developmentally appropriate.

Strong Moral and Social Values

We cultivate respect, discipline, teamwork, and responsibility in every learner. Our character education programme helps build ethical individuals with positive behaviour and social awareness.
