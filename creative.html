Creative Arts Curriculum

Nurturing imagination, expression & creativity in PP1 to Grade 4 learners through integrated arts education aligned with CBC standards.

Explore Curriculum

Areas Covered in Creative Arts

Drawing & Painting

Exploring colors, shapes, and techniques to express ideas visually using various mediums and tools.

Music

Singing, rhythm activities, introduction to simple instruments, and music appreciation from diverse cultures.

Drama

Role-play, storytelling, puppet shows, and simple performances to build confidence and communication skills.

Craft

Hands-on activities using recycled materials, paper, clay, and textiles to create 3D artworks and functional items.

Movement

Creative dance, coordination exercises, expressive body movements, and rhythm-based physical activities.

Collaboration

Group projects, mural painting, and ensemble performances that teach teamwork and appreciation of diverse perspectives.

Importance of Creative Arts

Builds Imagination & Self-expression

Creative arts provide children with a safe space to explore their thoughts, feelings, and ideas without judgment. It helps them develop their unique voice and creative thinking skills.

Encourages Teamwork & Confidence

Through group performances, collaborative projects, and peer feedback, children learn to work together, respect different perspectives, and build confidence in sharing their work.

Develops Fine Motor Skills

Activities like cutting, drawing, painting, and modeling strengthen hand-eye coordination, dexterity, and precision - essential skills for writing and other academic tasks.

Promotes Cultural Appreciation

Exposure to diverse art forms, music, dance, and stories from different cultures fosters global awareness, tolerance, and appreciation for cultural diversity.

Enhances Academic Performance

Arts education improves focus, memory, and problem-solving skills. Studies show that students engaged in arts perform better across all academic subjects.

Fosters Emotional Intelligence

Through arts, children learn to identify, express, and manage emotions. They develop empathy by understanding characters in stories and expressing feelings through art.

Learning Outcomes by Level

PP1 - PP2: Foundational Exploration

Early childhood creative development through play and sensory exploration

Color & Shape Recognition

Exploring primary colors and basic shapes through finger painting and collage

Rhythm & Song

Singing simple songs, nursery rhymes, and learning basic rhythm with percussion instruments

Role-play & Imagination

Basic role-play, imaginative games, and puppet shows to develop social skills

Free Drawing

Free drawing with crayons, markers, and chalk on different surfaces

Movement Coordination

Simple rhythm and movement activities to develop coordination and body awareness

Sensory Development

Introduction to textures through sensory play with various materials

Grade 1 - 2: Skill Development

Building foundational skills in various art forms with guided instruction

Mixed Media Art

Creative drawing with mixed media including watercolors, pastels, and charcoal

3D Art Forms

Basic modeling with clay and playdough to create simple 3D forms

Music Ensembles

Rhythm patterns, simple percussion ensembles, and introduction to melody

Short Performances

Short group performances, skits, and storytelling with simple costumes

Cultural Arts

Introduction to art from local culture through traditional crafts and music

Collaborative Projects

Collaborative murals and group art projects focusing on community themes

Grade 3 - 4: Creative Expression

Advanced exploration and development of personal artistic style

Design Principles

Introduction to design principles, perspective, and composition in visual arts

Melodic Instruments

Playing simple melodic instruments (recorder, xylophone) and reading basic notation

Script Writing & Performance

Storytelling, script writing, character development, and stage techniques

Cultural Dance Forms

Cultural dance forms, choreography, and performance skills

Art Appreciation

Art critique, appreciation sessions, and learning about famous artists

Digital Arts Introduction

Digital arts introduction using simple drawing software and creative apps
